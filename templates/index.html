Shamir Backup

What is Shamir's Secret Sharing?

Shamir's Secret Sharing is a cryptographic algorithm that allows you to split a secret (in this case, your cryptocurrency seed) into multiple shares. The unique feature is that you can specify how many shares are needed to reconstruct the original secret, providing both security and redundancy.

Key Features

  • Secure seed splitting using Shamir's Secret Sharing
  • QR code generation for easy share distribution
  • Time-locked shares for enhanced security
  • Multiple cryptocurrency seed format support

Supported Cryptocurrency Seeds

  • BIP39 Mnemonic (12, 15, 18, 21, or 24 words)
  • Electrum Seeds (12 or 24 words)
  • Hexadecimal Format
  • Base58 Format

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption of shares
  • Optional time-locking mechanism
  • Threshold-based reconstruction
  • Secure storage of encrypted shares

Terms of Use

Important Considerations:
  • Always backup your shares in multiple secure locations
  • Keep your shares separate and secure
  • Never share all shares with a single person
  • Test reconstruction with a small amount first
Security Warnings:
  • Use a secure, private connection
  • Verify share recipients carefully
  • Consider time-locking sensitive shares
  • Keep threshold requirements appropriate
